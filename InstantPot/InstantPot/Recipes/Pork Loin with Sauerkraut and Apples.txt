#NAME#
Pork Loin with Sauerkraut and Apples

2 pound pork loin roast
0.5 teaspoon Pink Himalayan salt
0.5 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons butter
2 large onions
3 cloves garlic
1 cup chicken bone broth

3 Granny Smith apples peeled, cored and chopped
