#NAME#
Speedy Sweet Potato Curry

1 yellow onion chopped
1 tbsp curry powder
1 tsp ground ginger
3 cups Water
2 sweet potatoes cut into 1-inch chunks
2 carrots peeled and chopped
1 cup red lentils
1.5 tsp fine sea salt
freshly ground black pepper 0.5 cup full fat coconut milk
1 cup chopped kale
1 tbsp pure maple syrup
Cooked rice, quinoa, or Easy Cauliflower “Rice” for serving chopped fresh cilantro for garnish  #LINK#
