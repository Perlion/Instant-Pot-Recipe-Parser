#NAME#
Sweet Potato Hummus
#INGREDIENTS#
2 tbsp avocado oil
1 sweet potato (large) peeled and cut into cubes
0.5 tsp salt
3 garlic cloves minced
1 large lemon the juice from the lemon is needed
15 oz cooked chickpeas
0.25 cup tahini
1 tsp ground cumin
