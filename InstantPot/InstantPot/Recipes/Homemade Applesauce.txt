#NAME#
Homemade Applesauce
#INGREDIENTS#
12 apples peeled, cored and quartered (Fuji apples are delicious)
2 tbsp  butter
1 tbsp cinnamon
1 tbsp honey
3 tbsp lemon juice
0.25 tsp kosher salt
0.75 cup Water
cinnamon  and/or cinnamon sticks for garnish (Additional option)  #LINK#
https://recipes.instantpot.com/recipe/homemade-applesauce/
#TIME#
45
